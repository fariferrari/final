New NU President: Renowned International Expert

Date: 2024-11-07 | Category: Education

Nazarbayev University (NU) has announced its new president, a distinguished expert known for their contributions to international education and research.

The new president has a proven track record in academia and has led various initiatives focused on academic excellence and global collaboration.

This appointment is expected to bring significant positive changes to NU, reinforcing its reputation as a top institution in Kazakhstan and Central Asia.

The new leadership aims to introduce innovative educational practices and strengthen ties with prestigious universities worldwide.
